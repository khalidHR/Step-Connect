✅ 1. Professional BRD – StepConnect (Refined from Social Steps concept)
1. Business Overview

StepConnect is a mobile-first social fitness application that transforms daily walking into an engaging and competitive experience. The system tracks user steps automatically and connects users with friends to participate in challenges and leaderboards.

The platform focuses on motivation through social interaction, making fitness simple, fun, and accessible.

2. Business Problem

Many individuals struggle to maintain consistent physical activity due to:

Lack of motivation
Absence of social accountability
Boring or complex fitness applications
Limited feedback on performance

Existing solutions either focus on tracking or social interaction, but not both in a simple and engaging way.

3. Business Objectives
Encourage daily movement through social competition
Provide automatic step tracking without user effort
Create a community-driven fitness experience
Improve engagement using leaderboards and challenges
Deliver a simple and accessible user experience
4. Key Features (From Real Analysis)

Based on your extracted data:

Core Features:
Automatic step tracking (no manual setup)
Social connection with friends
Leaderboard ranking system
Competitive challenges
Advanced Features:
Integration with wearable devices (Fitbit, Garmin)
Inclusive design (Wheelchair mode with adjusted calculation)
User Flow:
Download & Sign Up
Connect with friends
Start tracking and competing
5. Scope
In Scope:
Step tracking (Apple Health integration)
Leaderboard system
Friend connections
Basic dashboards (daily/weekly stats)
Simple challenges
Out of Scope:
Messaging system
Advanced AI recommendations
Multi-platform full support (PoC phase)
6. Functional Requirements

The system must:

Automatically retrieve step data
Allow users to connect with others
Rank users based on daily steps
Display leaderboard dynamically
Support basic challenges between users
Show progress in simple dashboards
7. Non-Functional Requirements
Usability: Very simple interface (no learning curve)
Performance: Real-time or near real-time updates
Reliability: Accurate step synchronization
Accessibility: Support different user conditions (e.g., wheelchair mode)
Scalability: Support increasing users over time
8. Business Value (This is your differentiator ⚠️)

This system provides value by:

Turning fitness into a social activity
Increasing consistency through competition psychology
Removing friction (automatic tracking)
Supporting inclusivity in fitness tracking

9. Assumptions
Users allow access to step data
Users are willing to connect socially
Mobile device sensors are accurate
10. Constraints
Limited to iOS (PoC phase)
Dependency on external APIs (Apple Health / wearables)
Limited dataset for testing real competition
11. Success Criteria
Users can track steps automatically
Leaderboard updates correctly
Social interaction increases engagement
System is easy to use without guidance
